About Me

Hi there! My name is Krishna and I have been teaching for the past 15 years. I am passionate about education and love sharing my knowledge with others.

In my classes, I specialize in teaching mathematics and coding. I work with both high school and undergraduate students, helping them develop their skills in subjects like C++, Python, and Java. I believe that understanding coding languages is not only essential for today's technological world but also a great way to develop problem-solving skills.

Aside from mathematics and coding, I also have a keen interest in history. I teach European and Indian history, providing my students with insights into the rich cultural heritage and significant events that have shaped our world.

When I'm not teaching, you can find me exploring new topics, reading books, or spending time with my family.
